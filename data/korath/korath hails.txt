# --- FILE STRUCTURE ---
# This file defines communication phrases for Korath Exiles and Kor Efret.
# Phrases are chosen based on ship state (hostile/friendly, disabled/abled) and faction.
# Prefixes indicate the language/style:
#   "efreti:"        = Kor Efret language phrase
#   "exile:"         = Korath Exile language phrase
#   "exile humanika:"= Severely mispronounced human phrases spoken by Korath Exiles
#   "exile mixed:"   = Contains a mix of Exile and mispronounced human words.
# Numbers after phrases/words are relative probabilities (weights). Higher numbers are more likely.
# Placeholders like [cargo] or [system] are filled in by the game engine.

# --- LORE NOTES ---
# Exiles speak broken human learned from raids, lacking proper grammar.
# Communication is basic (demands, threats, boasts), not complex negotiation.
# Efret stick to their own language, appearing more aloof or formal.
# Cultural references (food, items) reflect Korath biology and exiled status.
